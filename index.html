Welcome

I am an assistant professor in Quantitative Economics at the University of Potsdam, Germany. I obtained my PhD in 2022 from Universität Hamburg under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Miriam Beblo after graduating from an interdisciplinary Master's Program in Economics and Sociology. My main research interests are the Economics of Migration, Labour, and Gender, Language Economics, Applied Microeconometrics and Experimental Economics.

Besides scientific conferences and workshops, I occasionally give talks and presentations about gender, migration, and labour market topics to non-academic audiences.
